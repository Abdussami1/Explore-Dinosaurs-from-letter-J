Jaxartosaurus

Type of dinosaur: Large ornithopod
Length: 9.0m
Diet: Herbivorous
When it lived: Late Cretaceous, 94-84 million years ago
Found in: Kazakhstan
Jaxartosaurus is known only from a skull roof and braincase.

Jingshanosaurus

Type of dinosaur: Prosauropod
Length: 5.0m
Diet: Herbivorous
When it lived: Early Jurassic, 205-190 million years ago
Found in: China
A complete skeleton and skull of Jingshanosaurus have been found. It was one of the last prosauropods.

Jinzhousaurus

Type of dinosaur: Large ornithopod
Diet: Herbivorous
When it lived: Early Cretaceous, 127-121 million years ago
Found in: China
An almost complete skull and some postcranial remains of this dinosaur have been found.

Jobaria

Type of dinosaur: Sauropod
Length: 21.0m
Diet: Herbivorous
When it lived: Early Cretaceous, 132-94 million years ago
Found in: Niger
Jobaria is known from a skull and several skeletons.

Juravenator

Type of dinosaur: Small theropod
Length: 0.8m
Diet: Carnivorous
When it lived: Late Jurassic, 154-151 million years ago
Found in: Germany
Juravenator was the second non-avian (not bird-like) theropod (small meat-eating dinosaur) to be found in the region of Solnhofen.
The type specimen shows impressions of scaly skin along the tail.
